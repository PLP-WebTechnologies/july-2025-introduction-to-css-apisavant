Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow by Gabrielle Zevin

⭐ Review: A love letter to friendship, creativity, and video games. It's not a romance, but it feels like one in how it captures the intimacy of two people building worlds together. Emotional, nostalgic, and quietly brilliant.

Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros

⭐ Review: Dragons + deadly trials + slow-burn romance = BookTok's darling. It's fast-paced, dramatic, and unapologetically addictive. If you loved Hunger Games or ACOTAR, this feels like their fiery cousin.
